About Us

        We are a team of passionate computer engineering students from Pune Vidyarthi Griha College of Engineering in Pune, India. Our team has come together to develop a healthcare app that we believe will make a positive impact in people's lives. Through our shared vision and dedication, we have created a user-friendly platform that provides access to healthcare resources and services. Our goal is to revolutionize the healthcare industry by bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers. We are excited to bring our skills and knowledge to the table to make a difference in the world.

Mission and vision

  • To improve access to quality healthcare services for people all over the world through innovative and affordable technology solutions.
  • To create a healthcare application that is user-friendly and accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or level of technological proficiency.
  • To collaborate with healthcare professionals, policymakers, and other stakeholders to create a healthcare ecosystem that is more efficient, effective, and patient-centered.
